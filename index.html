i18n Sample Data Download page

This page contains links to sample data for testing Unicode. Data were gathered from open sources, modified and simplified for your convenience. Local names may be not official, sometimes they don't even correspond their real city and country names. Data can be used for testing only, not intendent for navigation or any other real life tasks and workflows.

Small dataset

Only 126 points with Unicode characters in “Name_Local” and “Country_Local” attributes. Has some quontitive data in "POP" and "POP_RANK" fields that can be used to display graphs and charts.

Large dataset

This is a point dataset containing over 13K points with Unicode characters in attributes. Many points are fake and created just to display some local characters in "name" and "Country" fields.
